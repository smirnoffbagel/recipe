Pasta Carbonara

Home

Description

Delicious Carbonara pasta with savory homemade sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Your choice, one box of noodles
  • Pack of Bacon
  • Half a jar of pickeled jalapenos (could use fresh if preferred)
  • Whole yellow onion
  • 6 Eggs
  • Half block of shredded parmesan (pre-shredded bag works just fine)
  • Half a stick of butter

Steps:

  1. Cut bacon into fine strips (or diced) cooking on low to medium heat until starting to brown.
  2. Finely dice the onion, adding to pan with butter on medium heat; until caramelized.
  3. While they are cooking seperate the yolks from your eggs and mix with the parmesan until it begins to resemble a paste.
  4. Set the noodles up to boil once the bacon, onions, and jalapenos are close to being done (give yourself approx. 10 min)
  5. Save about a quarter to half a cup of the noodle water to add into the mixture.
  6. Finally, add everyhting together in a bowl and mix well until the egg and parm paste becomes a sauce.
  7. At last you can enjoy!
