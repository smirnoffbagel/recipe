Traditional Style Pizza

Home

Ingredients:

  • Store bought pizza dough
  • Store bought sauce
  • Fresh pepperoni from the store
  • Fresh mozzarrella pre-shredded in a bag

Steps:

  1. Throw some flour on that dough and hit it bash it and spread it
  2. Push down and spread dough in circular motion until stretched out
  3. Take your fresh sauce and drizzle on your crust
  4. Sprinkle cheese on
  5. Add as many pepperoni as you like
  6. Pop it in the oven at 400 for 10 minutes and enjoy!
